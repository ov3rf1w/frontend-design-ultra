# Website Type Playbooks

Use this when a domain token or brief implies a website type. Defaults can be overridden by the user's brand and assets.

| Type | Hero logic | Trust logic | CTA logic | Rhythm | Motion budget | Asset needs | Premium move | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Agency/studio | craft proof or featured case first | case outcomes, logos, team credibility | start project, see cases | hero, featured case, offer, process, proof, CTA | mid | case visuals, process, portraits | case-lens reveal | generic portfolio wall |
| SaaS | outcome + product state | integrations, compliance, users, uptime | trial/demo | hero, workflow, proof, pricing, FAQ | mid | screenshots, UI states | product object stage | feature dump first |
| B2B services | promise + qualification | credentials, process, case proof | discovery call/download | why, how, proof, process, trust | low-mid | team/process/proof | sticky process chapter | overpromising |
| Local business | location + availability | reviews, map, service area | call/directions/booking | short dense practical bands | low | local photos, map, staff | local proof board | national-template feel |
| Hospitality | mood + booking intent | ratings, direct booking, amenities | check availability/explore | cinematic, rooms, proof, amenities | low-mid | property/food/place media | editorial image/type frame | slow value decode |
| Portfolio/showroom | work/result first | press, awards, outcomes | view case/book consult | media, process, quote, CTA | low-mid | high-res work | full-bleed case chapter | rigid gallery grid |
| Ecommerce | product/category intent | reviews, shipping, returns, security | buy/add/configure | product, value, proof, bundle, policy | low | product shots, variants | variant stage | animated promo clutter |
| Dashboard/app | active data state | reliability, integrations, privacy | launch/demo | command, insight, detail, action | low-mid | real UI states | density choreography | marketing-card dashboard |
| Legal/finance/healthcare | calm clarity + next step | licenses, safeguards, process | secure consult/download | trust, proof, process, safeguards | low | documents, people, diagrams | evidence board | fear tactics |
| Luxury/editorial | singular statement + art media | provenance, press, detail | private session/collection | airy, fewer sections | mid | premium photography/materials | immersive footer | SaaS card rhythm |
| Startup/launch | thesis + status | validation, roadmap, milestones | join list/demo | tease, proof, roadmap, urgency | mid-high bounded | launch assets, demo | timeline reveal | fake traction |

## Universal Type Selection

When type is unspecified, choose the playbook's default type direction, then justify it. Do not fall back to generic system fonts for premium-facing pages unless the domain demands product familiarity.

## Universal Asset Selection

If required assets are absent, choose a different visual carrier. Typography/proof/interface composition is better than weak stock atmosphere.

## Universal Motion Selection

Motion defaults to the playbook budget. Increase only when the signature move needs it and gates remain intact.
