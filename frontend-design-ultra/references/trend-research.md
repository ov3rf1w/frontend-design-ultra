# Trend Research

Use for `/trend`, `/awwward`, premium landing pages, portfolio/campaign work, and redesigns that need modern reference intelligence.

## Research Targets

Use public inspiration sources when browsing is allowed: award galleries, curated web design directories, portfolio showcases, product launches, editorial sites, and high-quality brand sites.

## Extract Mechanics

For each reference, capture:

- first viewport composition
- type treatment
- color/surface system
- media/asset role
- interaction model
- scroll rhythm
- signature moment
- conversion/proof placement
- mobile adaptation guess
- performance risk

## Synthesis

Return:

- 3-5 reusable mechanics
- 3 overused patterns to avoid
- one recommended design lane
- one signature move
- asset requirements
- motion constraints
- verification risks

Never copy a reference's brand, copy, exact layout, logo, or proprietary visuals.
