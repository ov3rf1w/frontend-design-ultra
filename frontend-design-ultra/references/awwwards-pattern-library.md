# Awwwards Pattern Library

Use for `/awwward`, `/premium`, `/landing`, `/portfolio`, `/trend`, and creative redesigns.

## Editorial Hero

Use when brand voice, photography, or typography is the product.

- Sketch: oversized type, strong image crop, restrained nav, one quiet action.
- Mobile: keep type readable; preserve image subject.
- Risk: weak if assets are generic.

## Kinetic Typography

Use when message and rhythm carry the experience.

- Sketch: split phrase into timed or scroll-bound lines; animate transform/opacity only.
- Mobile: reduce to staggered static or subtle reveal.
- Risk: illegible copy or motion hiding message.

## Scroll Chapters

Use for process, story, product mechanism, or timeline.

- Sketch: anchored chapters, one changing axis per chapter, visible progress.
- Mobile: stacked narrative; no forced pinning.
- Risk: blank pinned states and slow reading.

## Image-Mask Reveal

Use for fashion, architecture, hospitality, product detail, editorial reveals.

- Sketch: clip-path or mask reveals image while copy remains readable.
- Mobile: simpler crop reveal or static frame.
- Risk: mask animation causing jank or hiding focal point.

## Object-Stage Product Hero

Use when a product, app state, 3D object, or physical object can be staged.

- Sketch: product centered as stage object; supporting UI/proof orbits around it.
- Mobile: object first, proof below.
- Risk: fake product screenshots.

## Immersive Footer

Use when the ending should feel like a brand moment.

- Sketch: oversized type, final CTA, location/contact/proof, strong surface shift.
- Mobile: direct action first, brand ending below.
- Risk: footer becomes decorative and hides useful links.

## Proof Board

Use for B2B, SaaS, services, trust-heavy offers.

- Sketch: metrics, logos, quotes, case snippets arranged as evidence.
- Mobile: prioritize strongest proof first.
- Risk: fake logos or unsupported numbers.

## Interactive Index

Use for services, product categories, portfolios, or case lists.

- Sketch: large list rows with hover/focus preview and quick context.
- Mobile: tap-to-expand cards.
- Risk: hover-only interaction with no touch equivalent.

## Spatial Cards

Use for feature sets only when depth or layering clarifies hierarchy.

- Sketch: cards arranged by importance, not equal grid.
- Mobile: ordered stack with persistent hierarchy.
- Risk: bento grid without content logic.

## Cursor-Follow Preview

Use for portfolio indexes or editorial lists on desktop.

- Sketch: lightweight image/metadata preview follows cursor on hover-capable devices.
- Mobile: inline preview or tap expansion.
- Risk: inaccessible hover-only core content.
